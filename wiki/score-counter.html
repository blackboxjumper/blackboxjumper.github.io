Score counter was introduced in 1.3 Test build 2.
Although it is fully functional and you can get and lose points, it was never used for anything and never mentioned in the tutorial.
The score counter was removed in 1.4 snapshot 3a.


How it works

You get coins by collectiong keys and finishing the levels.
You lose points for dying and killing the enemies.
